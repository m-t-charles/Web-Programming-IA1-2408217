AI Business Idea Report: Personalized Fitness and Nutrition Plans

After reading through the AI Business Ideas of 2025 clog, the idea that stuck out to me was the AI-powered fitness app. It would use the AI systems to calculate what lifestyle would be best for the user to leave healthy.

Application in My World

As some struggling with chronic diseases themself, the app could greatly benefit me to learn how to live healthy with my condition. It would also help those who can't see a nutritionalist for numerous reasons.

Ethical Considerations

A key ethical issue to consider is that it violates many laws for those unrelated to certain medical fields to see one's medical documents. Even wit giving permission, the databases being seen b anyone else would be a huge violation.

Proposed Solutions

To address these concerns, I would ensure that the data for the users would stay encrypted with the actual database itself.

An existing app in this space is Fitness AI, which uses adaptive technology to personalize fitness workouts and meals.
